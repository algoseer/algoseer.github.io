About

Hi! I am a Ph.D. student at the SAIL lab at USC. My research interests lie in the field of Signal Processing/ Machine Learning. While the diverse nature of my work allows for exploration of fields not traditionally considered Signal Processing, it often also inspires new ideas that can only come as a result of thinking out of the box.

I received my Bachelor of Technology degree from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, India in 2009, where I trained to be an Instrumentation engineer. I am originally from Kolkata, India.
